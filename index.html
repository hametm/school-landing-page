Who We Are

We learn by doing. Our curriculum pushes students to go beyond current boundaries by using concrete forms and materials, rather than relying solely on abstract theories and preconceptions. Students design, build, and inhabit structures out in the Arizona desert, drawing on the vision of the school's founder, Frank Lloyd Wright.
